Vattanac Bank is a Cambodian-owned bank offering retail and commercial banking services. Its head office is located at Level 2, Vattanac Capital, a landmark building in Phnom Penh. The Bank commenced operations on 8 November 2002, Vattanac Bank has 38 branches: 14 in Phnom Penh and 24 in the Provinces.
