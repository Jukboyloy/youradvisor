Wing Bank (Cambodia) Plc, the bank for every Cambodian, is driven by the vision of providing convenient access to digital financial services to enhance the daily lives of all Cambodians. Since its inception on 18 August 2008, Wing Bank has been a trailblazer, transforming the way Cambodians access financial services by introducing instant, secure, and convenient Mobile Financial Services.


Presently, Wing Bank ensures complete coverage of all districts in Cambodia, reaching the entire Cambodian population. This achievement is made possible through the innovative Wing Bank App, a network of over 15,000 Wing Cash Express agents, collaboration with more than 55,000 merchants, and strategic partnerships with industry leaders such as Mastercard, Visa, UnionPay International, MoneyGram, AliPay, WeChat Pay, Western Union, Ria, and many more.

Committed to providing financial, gender, and digital inclusion, Wing Bank caters to the unbanked and the under-banked, ensuring that every Cambodian can enjoy the convenience and security offered by modern financial services.
