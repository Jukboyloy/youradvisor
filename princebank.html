Prince Bank Plc. started its business operations in 2015 as a private microfinance institution under the former name “Prince Finance Plc.” and was transformed into a commercial bank with license No. B 46 was granted by the National Bank of Cambodia on 18 July 2018.


Currently, Prince Bank Plc. has thirty-five branches located in twenty-two provinces and cities including Phnom Penh (Head Office and 11 other locations), Battambang, Siem Reap (2 locations), Kampong Cham, Preah Sihanouk, Krong Ta Khmao, Prey Veng, Svay Rieng, Kratie, Rattanakiri, Kampong Chhnang, Preah Vihear, Takeo, Kampong Speu, Kampong Thom, Krong Poi Pet, Banteay Meanchey, Tbong Khmum, Pursat, Kampot, Stoeng Treng and Mondolkiri.

We are keen on contributing to developing small and medium enterprises that constitute a key segment of our target customers.
